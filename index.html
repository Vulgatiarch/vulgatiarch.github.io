What This Is

This site exists to present the collection of homebrew from the team of the Vulgatiam to the general public, so that our creations might see the light of day and have an existence beyond ourselves. Add to the history of our chapters and cults, and even submit your stories for official sanction on our Discord. The priority is cultivated content that is in full compliance with official canon material. Content presented here is unofficial and not sanctioned by Games Workshop.
